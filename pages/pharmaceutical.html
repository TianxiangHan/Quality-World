GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice)

Quality assurance system ensuring pharmaceutical products are consistently produced and controlled according to quality standards.

🏭

GDP (Good Distribution Practice)

Guidelines ensuring the quality and integrity of pharmaceutical products throughout the distribution network.

🚚

GCP (Good Clinical Practice)

International ethical and scientific quality standard for designing, conducting, and reporting clinical trials.

🧪

ICH Guidelines

International harmonization guidelines for technical requirements of pharmaceuticals for human use.

🌐

FDA Regulations

US Food and Drug Administration regulations governing pharmaceutical development, manufacturing, and marketing.

🇺🇸

EMA Guidelines

European Medicines Agency guidelines for pharmaceutical authorization and supervision in the European Union.

🇪🇺

USP Standards

United States Pharmacopeia standards providing quality criteria for medicines, food ingredients, and dietary supplements.
